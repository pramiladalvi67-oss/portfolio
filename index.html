How It Works

From Raw Snap to Premium Ad

A five-stage neural pipeline that turns any product photo into advertising-grade visuals.

STEP 01

RAW INPUT

CAPTURE

Client sends any smartphone or DSLR photo of their product—no special requirements, no staging needed.

STEP 02

AI ANALYSIS

SCANNING

Our AI pipeline scans the product for geometry, surface material, light conditions and chromatic artifacts.

STEP 03

IMAGE ENHANCEMENT

UPSCALING

We upscale details, enhance textures, balance ambient exposure, and clean up chromatic distortions or artifacts.

STEP 04

SCENE GENERATION

STAGING

Generative lighting synthesis constructs premium aesthetic surroundings matching the product's design language.

STEP 05

FINAL ADVERTISEMENT

OUTPUT

The final ultra-premium, high-resolution advertising visual is generated, graded for digital display, and delivered.
